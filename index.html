Coworking Spaces Built For Your Start Startups

Thiel spends 6 months writing down everything he knows about business. His book sells for $10 on Amazon. Adam Wathan and Steve Schoger spend 6 months writing down everything they know about design. Their “book” sells for $79 online.Believe it or not, both prices make perfect sense.Thiel is selling to the masses and the masses are sensitive to price changes (price elastic). If he raises the price to $30 they wouldn’t think twice before substituting his book for one of the many cheaper alternatives on Amazon.
